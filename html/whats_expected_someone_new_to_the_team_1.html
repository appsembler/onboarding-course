If I'm new to the team, I will

  1. Not commit to more than 3 points of tasks during my first week, or 5 points my second week (so no more than a total of 8 points for the first sprint). It's really easy to over-commit at first, so keep your commitments manageable. You can always take on more work later in the sprint if you finish early.
  2. Provide lots of updates to my assigned reviewer/mentor for each task, so they can provide timely help.
