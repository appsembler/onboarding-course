While the weekly sprint meetings will let you talk to your co-workers face-to-face, they don't give you much opportunity to get to know people personally.

As a newcomer you have the option to get to know people better via short introductory one-to-one video calls. After you've attended two or more sprint meetings you should have some idea about what everyone's working on so you will be better placed to start having these conversations.

You don't have to talk about work, or coding or anything in particular. Your co-workers are spread across the globe and have led vastly different lives there is a lot to learn from each other.

While a video call lets you talk face-to-face, it is not mandatory. You could choose to make an audio-only call, or have a text chat. You can look at OC-3328 for more information and guidance.
