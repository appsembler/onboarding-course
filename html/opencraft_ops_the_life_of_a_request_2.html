Getting an Open edX Instance

Before one can make an HTTP request to anything, they need a valid instance. Customers usually get this by going to http://opencraft.com/hosting/ and picking an option. Let's assume they chose the Pro & Teacher option.

By default, when registering for an Open edX instance through the Ocim registration process, you choose a subdomain under opencraft.hosting, e.g. my-openedx-instance.opencraft.hosting, provided that it isn't already taken by somebody else.

Alternatively, customers can chose to configure their own domain, such as "subdomain.example.com". The explanation for setting the DNS records for this are provided during the registration process.
