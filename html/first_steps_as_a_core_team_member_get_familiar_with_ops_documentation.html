As a core team member, you are expected to be capable of taking on DevOps-y tasks. You'll likely be involved in SSHing into machines, looking at graphs of metrics of our infrastructure, performing root cause analyses, deploying new servers, configuring machines with Ansible, and so on. Hence it's important you get familiar with our infrastructure. The newcomer section already includes the link to the Ops documentation but it is encouraged to revisit it now.

It is also recommended to go through the list of maintained instances that we host and ensure you have access to the all the instances except for those which have restricted access due to security reasons. Please reach out the respective epic owners or your mentor for the required access.
