Prior to your arrival on the team, we will arrange for a core team member to be your mentor. We will also arrange your accounts and access to Github/Gitlab, JIRA, and our communication tools.

If you find a link in the course or related materials that you're unable to access, please check that:

  • You are logged into the site using appropriate credentials.
  • You have access to the linked repository.
  • The page/repository still exists.
