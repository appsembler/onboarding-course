We use Hashicorp Vault to manage and store all the SSH keys and credentials used for production and stage environments.

We also store the secrets required for deploying a client Open edX instance in separate, private repositories and the access to those will be provided on an as-needed basis. Other secrets for deploying our servers are stored in the ansible-secrets repository. Ask the task reviewer or your mentor if you need access to any of these repositories for a task.

Please read Secrets access and set up your accounts for these services. You might need to ask for help from your onboarding ticket reviewer to get access.

Note that for some servers (namely Ocim) you'll still need to ask someone with access in order to authorize your key.
