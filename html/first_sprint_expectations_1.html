As a newcomer you should:

  1. Not commit to more than 3 points of tasks during your first week, or 5 points your second week (so 8 points in a two-week sprint). It's really easy to over-commit at first, so keep your commitments manageable. If you finish early you can always ask in the Sprint planning channel whether someone needs any help, and take on more work later in the sprint.
  2. Provide lots of updates to your assigned reviewer/mentor for each task, so they can provide timely help.

What's expected of your first sprint:

  1. Make sure to reply to pings/emails within 24 hours.
  2. Plan your time to work the contracted amount of hours.
  3. Understand the task worflow by reviewing Task Statuses.
  4. Watch for tasks marked as Newcomer Friendly to assign yourself for the next sprint.
