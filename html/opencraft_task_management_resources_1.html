Taking care of business: resources for task management

As a senior developer, you probably have methods and strategies for planning and executing your work. At OpenCraft, task planning is taken very seriously - we give it a lot of emphasis because careful task planification is an excellent way to deliver your work while maintaining crunch time, spillover and chronic stress at bay. We believe the effort spent doing proper planning is entirely worth it, as it will help you perform better and maintain a healthier life-work balance.
