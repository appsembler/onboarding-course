As you work on tickets you will start receiving emails about them. To reduce the number of notifications:

  • Use Gmail to filter out emails, put them into folders, etc.
  • Unsubscribe from JIRA tickets and Github notifications that do not relate to what you're working on.
  • Disable notifications (such as your phone) while you are working.

You can set up JIRA to send an email only if your @username is mentioned. Because other people (including your reviewer) might have set it up in this way, always mention the username when you address them directly, so that they be notified.
