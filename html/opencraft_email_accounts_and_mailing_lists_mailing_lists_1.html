Please read the docs on mailing-lists and subscribe yourself to the relevant mailing-lists with your @opencraft.com e-mail.

Note that if you are a newcomer you only need to subscribe to help@opencraft.com and urgent@opencraft.com if you need to access client communication in a task.

For example, if you want to subscribe on the help@opencraft.com, you should first go to https://mail.plebia.org/cgi-bin/mailman/listinfo/help and fill in the fields on "Subscribing to help" section. Then, a core team member will approve it and you have access to the email from that list.
