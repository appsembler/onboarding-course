We use an internally-developed application called Sprints to help us manage our time commitments during the sprint planning sessions.

Have a look at your cell's Sprint Planning Report and make sure it shows the correct number of hours under "Goal" (how much time you'll have to work) for the upcoming two-week sprint. This number should be lower by 2 than your contracted number of hours, because 2 hours are reserved for the weekly sprint meetings.

This dashboard also shows your tasks that need to be estimated before the sprint planning meeting.

If you'd like to know more about how Sprints works, please visit the docs.
